Adventures Worth Having

I believe the best things in life are experiential. Every day should be a new adventure, whether it's a normal day at work, a trip to the grocery store, a hike with friends, or an international excursion, every situation has beauty that should be captured.

Travel The World

When you think of traveling, what's the first thing that comes into your head? For me, it's 3 things:

  1. People
  2. Places
  3. Food

I've had the privelege of traveling to a variety of places around the world, and this website is dedicated to sharing some of those adventures, including to:

  • Israel
  • Jordan
  • Egypt
  • Taiwan
  • Germany
  • Scotland
  • Ecuador
  • Mexico

When I travel, I focus on the following:

  • Exploring Places
  • Learning about Culture
  • Meeting People
  • Experiencing Traditions (especially food!)
