How it All Got Started

My aunt first gave me a camera when I was around 10 years old. I still have it somewhere. That little camera represented the beginning of a lifetime loving travel, adventure, and photography.

Although photography is only a hobby, I hope to be able to share it with the world as I continue to learn and capture more experiences!
